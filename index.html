About Us

Ananda. Dive N’ Flow is a diving and ocean adventure center based in Santa Teresa, Costa Rica. We offer scuba diving, freediving, snorkeling, and private boat tours with a focus on small groups, personalized experiences, and safety.

Our team blends professional instruction with a warm, relaxed atmosphere—helping you connect with the ocean and explore the rich marine life around Santa Teresa and Malpaís.

  • Small groups & personal attention
  • Safety‑first briefings & top‑notch gear
  • Friendly, knowledgeable crew

Activities & Tours

Scuba Diving

Guided reef dives for all levels. Clear briefings, small groups, and vibrant marine life sightings.

Freediving

Breath‑hold techniques, safety, and coached sessions designed to build confidence and comfort.

Snorkeling

Family‑friendly coastal snorkel trips—shallow reefs, colorful fish, and calm bays.

Whale‑watching

Seasonal boat tours to spot whales and dolphins around Santa Teresa & Malpaís.
